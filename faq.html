Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Omniflow, your AI-powered product management tool.

Getting Started

What is Omniflow?

Omniflow is an AI-powered platform that automates the entire product development lifecycle, from ideation to release. It helps teams streamline their workflows through AI agents and customizable workflows.

How does Omniflow work?

Omniflow uses advanced AI agents to automate tasks like documentation, sprint planning, technical design, and more. It integrates seamlessly with your existing tools and adapts to your team's workflow.

Features & Capabilities

What features does Omniflow offer?

Omniflow offers Ideaflow for brainstorming, Docflow for documentation, Devflow for development planning, and Projectflow for project management. Each module is designed to work together seamlessly.

Can I customize the workflows?

Yes, Omniflow's workflows are fully customizable to match your team's specific processes. You can adapt each step to fit your organization's needs.

Integration & Compatibility

Does Omniflow integrate with other tools?

Yes, Omniflow integrates with popular tools like JIRA, Asana, and more. You can continue using your existing tools while leveraging Omniflow's AI capabilities.

Can I use Omniflow with my current tools?

Absolutely! Omniflow is designed to work alongside your existing tools. You don't need to change your workflows to use Omniflow.

Team & Collaboration

How does team collaboration work in Omniflow?

Omniflow provides real-time updates, clear documentation, and integrated workflows that bring together engineering, QA, and design teams.

Can I invite my team to use Omniflow?

Yes, you can easily invite team members to your projects and share reports with them. Omniflow supports team collaboration across different roles and departments.
