Frequently Asked Questions

How does Omniflow know my team's development velocity?

Omniflow enables your team/admin to adjust the development velocity for each team member, ensuring that workflows and plans are tailored to your team.

A story point may mean different things from one team to another. How does Omniflow know what it means for my team?

You can easily set a baseline for your team's velocity and define what a story point means by adjusting the "Development Velocity" settings.

My team is already using JIRA. Do we have to drop JIRA to start using Omniflow?

No, you don't have to drop JIRA to start using Omniflow. Omniflow can integrate with JIRA, allowing your team to leverage both tools simultaneously for enhanced workflow management and efficiency.

How is Omniflow better than other task management tools such as JIRA, Asana, etc?

Omniflow automates your product development lifecycle by streamlining everything from ideation to release. It automates sprint planning, technical documentation, technical design, and resource management, reducing manual effort and the back-and-forth communications.
